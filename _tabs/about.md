Hello! Welcome to my site. I am a researcher and engineer specializing in the worlds of Computer Vision, Neural Machine Interfaces and Natural Language Processing.
I also pursue numerous side projects and hobbies that I find quite enjoyable which you can read about in the posts and publications tab. 

# Education
I hold a Master's degree in Computer Science from Columbia University, where I focused on Self-Supervised and Semi-Supervised Visual Learning under Dr. Richard Zemel. I was part of 10 students who were selected for the Advanced Research Specialization while at Columbia due to my research contributions. I earned my Bachelor's degree from Boston College, where I competed on the tennis team for four years and served as captain for the last two.

# Hobbies
